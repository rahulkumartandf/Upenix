About UpeNix

Leading IT Transformation for Financial Services

Our Story

Founded in 2018, UpeNix emerged from a simple vision: help mid-sized financial institutions navigate the complexity of digital transformation. Our founders, veterans of Fortune 500 tech companies, recognized the gap between enterprise-grade solutions and accessible consulting for growing firms.

Today, UpeNix has partnered with over 150+ financial services companies, managing infrastructure and security for assets exceeding $2 trillion in total value under management.
